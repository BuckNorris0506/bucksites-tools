# Reset water filter light on GE refrigerators

GE uses both **touch panels** and **physical pads**; the reset is always tied to the filter status function for your specific UI.

## Touch / “UltraFresh” style panels

1. Open the **Settings** or **Options** area on the dispenser.  
2. Choose **Water Filter** or **Filter** → **Reset** (or **Replace** then confirm).  
3. If a long-press is required, the manual will say which icon to hold and for how long.

## Older pad or “Customizer” layouts

Often: press and hold the **Water Filter** pad **5–10 seconds** until the indicator resets. Some units use **Lock** + **Water** in sequence—only use the sequence listed for your model.

## Bluetooth / SmartHQ models

If your fridge pairs with the **SmartHQ** app, filter status and reset steps may also appear there after the physical filter change.

## Troubleshooting

- Confirm filter model and installation match GE’s instructions for your refrigerator.  
- Run water until flow is steady.  
- Download the manual from [GE Appliances owner support](https://www.geappliances.com/ge/service-and-support/support.htm) by model number and search **water filter reset**.

---

*Never rely on a single third-party button map; GE’s manual for your model number is definitive.*
