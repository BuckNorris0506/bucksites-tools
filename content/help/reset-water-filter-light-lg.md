# Reset water filter light on LG refrigerators

LG uses a few different control layouts. The goal is always to tell the appliance you installed a new filter so the “replace filter” reminder turns off.

## Electronic panel with Water button

On many models with a **Water** dispensing control on the door:

1. Press and hold **Water** (sometimes labeled **Filter Reset** in the manual) for **about three seconds** until the filter indicator clears or the panel confirms reset.

If nothing changes, try the same on **Ice Type** or **Lock**, depending on your panel—LG documents vary by series.

## Dispenser paddle / simple LED models

Some units reset from the **dispenser area** or require a **combination** of **Ice Type + Water** (hold both)—again per model year.

## If it will not reset

- Confirm the new filter is fully seated and the correct part for your model.  
- Unplug the refrigerator for one minute, plug back in, then repeat the reset once.  
- Open your **owner’s manual** PDF from [LG support](https://www.lg.com/us/support) and search **filter reset** for your exact model number.

---

*Steps differ by model. The manual for your specific refrigerator is the accurate source.*
