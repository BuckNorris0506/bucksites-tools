# Reset water filter light on Samsung refrigerators

Samsung filter reminders are cleared from the **front panel** or **Family Hub** screen, depending on the product.

## Most dispenser panels

1. Find **Alarm / Hold 3 sec for Filter Reset** (or similar—labels vary).  
2. Press and hold **about three seconds** until the filter reminder clears.

On some models the control is under **Fridge Manager** or a **Settings** submenu on the same display.

## Family Hub models

Open the **Fridge Manager** app on the screen → **Overview** or **Water filter** → follow **Reset** or **Replace filter** instructions shown there.

## Internal controls (no door screen)

A few units reset via a **button combination** on an interior control (e.g., **Alarm** + **Freezer**) described only in that model’s manual—do not guess; look up the pair for your unit.

## Still stuck

- Verify the cartridge is the correct part and fully locked.  
- Dispense and discard several liters of water.  
- Check [Samsung support](https://www.samsung.com/us/support/) with your **full model code** for the reset section of the user guide.

---

*Samsung revises UI labels often; your downloaded user manual is the reliable reference.*
