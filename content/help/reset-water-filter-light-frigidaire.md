# Reset water filter light on Frigidaire refrigerators

Frigidaire and Electrolux-group models usually clear the filter reminder after installation using a **control panel** action defined for that series.

## Typical dispenser display

1. With a new filter installed and flushed, find **Options**, **Reset**, or a **filter** icon on the display.  
2. Press and hold the control called out in your manual—commonly **three to five seconds**—until the indicator turns off.

Some units require entering **Options** first, then selecting **Water Filter** → **Reset**.

## Models without a full display

Lower-feature models may use a **multi-function key** (e.g., **Freezer +** along with another key) per the printed user guide. Avoid trial-and-error; the wrong combo can change other settings.

## If the light stays on

- Open the filter cover and reseat the cartridge until it clicks or aligns with the diagram.  
- Dispense several batches of water.  
- Power-cycle once if the manual allows it, then repeat the documented reset.

## Official instructions

Use [Frigidaire support](https://www.frigidaire.com/en/support) (or the Electrolux site for sister brands) to download the guide for **your model** and search **filter reset**.

---

*Naming and steps differ across Frigidaire lines; the owner’s manual for your model is the only copy you should treat as exact.*
