# Reset water filter light on Whirlpool refrigerators

Whirlpool (and many closely related brands) usually reset the filter status from the **dispenser user interface** after a new cartridge is installed.

## Common door-panel method

1. Locate the **Filter Reset** control on the dispenser touch panel (wording varies: “Filter,” “Options,” or a dedicated reset soft key).  
2. Press and hold per the **chart in your manual**—often **three seconds**—until the filter status light or message turns off.

Some models use a **menu path** instead (e.g., **Options** → **Filter Reset**).

## Inside-the-fridge controls

French-door and some side-by-side units put filter status on an **in-door** or **upper** control. Look for **Measured Fill**, **Options**, or **Lock** combinations described in the manual under **water filter**.

## Aftermarket or stuck indicators

If the light stays on after a proper OEM-spec filter and full seating:

- Run a few gallons of water, then retry reset.  
- Power-cycle the refrigerator once, then repeat the reset sequence.

## Get the exact sequence

Enter your model number on [Whirlpool product support](https://www.whirlpool.com/services/manuals.html) and open the **Owner’s Manual** → search **filter reset**.
